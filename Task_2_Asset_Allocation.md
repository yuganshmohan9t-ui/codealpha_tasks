## Structural Investment Architecture Analysis
The allocation structures engineered above isolate core assets from market tracking gaps. 85% of capital allocations secure macro market beta across indexes and large-caps, insulating the primary layer against catastrophic corrections. The 15% combined satellite allocations (Midcaps + Crypto) capitalize on non-correlated upward momentum, structurally beating baseline inflation metrics (~4.7% CPI) without risking unhedged structural liquidations.
